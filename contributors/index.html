Industry Partners

5

Companies driving innovation in robotics and AI

Semio AI

Semio AI is the founding industry partner of the Semio Community, providing leadership, technical infrastructure, and innovative platforms that democratize access to social robotics and advance human-robot interaction through open science and reproducible research.

Industry

IK Studio

IK Studio is a design and innovation firm partnering with Semio Community to advance the integration of robotics into architectural and interactive environments, bridging the gap between artistic expression and technological functionality in human-robot interaction.

Industry

OLogic

OLogic, Inc. is a leading robotics engineering and manufacturing company partnering with Semio Community to develop, produce, and support advanced robotics hardware platforms, bringing research robots from concept to production with a focus on quality, reliability, and manufacturability.

Industry

Palaio Logic

Specializing in embedded software development for robotics and AI. Palaio Logic offers consulting for software architecture and SDK design.

Industry

Plover

Plover provides product testing and recommendations to help technology meet users' needs.

Industry

Non-Profit Partners

3

Organizations advancing social impact through technology

Peerbots

Peerbots is a nonprofit organization partnering with Semio Community to advance accessible robotics technologies and foster community-driven development in human-robot interaction, with a focus on democratizing access to social robotics.

Nonprofit

AI LA

A public benefit nonprofit organization supporting the science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics communities of the Greater Los Angeles area.

Nonprofit

KIPR

The KISS Institute for Practical Robotics is a nonprofit organization partnering with Semio Community to advance educational robotics, making robotics technology accessible to students and educators worldwide through innovative programs, competitions, and curriculum development.

Leading universities and research institutions

Academic

UPenn

The University of Pennsylvania is a world-renowned Ivy League institution partnering with Semio Community to advance robotics research through innovative mechanical design, modular robotics, and architectural robotics initiatives.

Academic

USC

Academic

Tufts

Tufts University is a premier research institution partnering with Semio Community to advance artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems, and human-AI collaboration, with particular emphasis on creating intelligent systems that can effectively understand and work alongside humans.

Academic

Yale

Academic

GMU
