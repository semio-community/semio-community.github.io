Our Mission

Advancing human-centered robotics and AI through community collaboration

Semio Community is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization facilitating community-driven robotics hardware, software, and research to foster reproducible, replicable, and generalizable science and reusable systems within human-robot interaction (HRI).

Community-Driven

Fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing across disciplines

Human-Centered

Prioritizing ethical and accessible robotics and AI technologies

Replicable

Promoting reproducible scientific research and reusable systems

Founded with the belief that the future of robotics lies in collaborative innovation, we work to break down barriers between research and application, making cutting-edge robotics technology accessible to researchers, developers, and educators worldwide.
