# Research Services: 6-Card Category Options

Each option below provides six category titles with one-line summaries suitable for use as card descriptions, aligned with the concise style used in the Hardware and Software sections.

---

- Grants — Identify opportunities, develop competitive proposals, and coordinate submissions for HRI funding.
- Broader Impacts — Design and deliver community-facing activities and artifacts that meet program requirements.
- Events — Plan, host, and support workshops, user studies, and symposia for the HRI community.
- Populations — Recruit diverse in-person and remote participants, including special populations, for HRI studies.
- Research Studies — Design and pre-register replicable protocols for lab, remote, and field evaluations.
- Analysis — Build robust pipelines for data cleaning, statistical modeling, and result visualization.
